About Profitability App

Run operations and profitability from one connected workspace

Profitability App helps teams manage products, orders, customers, and analytics in one place. Instead of switching between storefront tools, spreadsheets, and reporting dashboards, teams can work from a single platform with clear visibility into revenue, margins, and inventory impact.

Core platform areas

The application is designed so operations and business intelligence stay aligned as data changes.

Catalog & Inventory

Keep product records, stock levels, and reorder signals accurate with traceable updates.

Orders & Customers

Manage order lifecycles, customer history, and source filtering from one operational queue.

Analytics & Profitability

Track revenue, gross profit, net profit, and margins with storefront and manual source splits.
